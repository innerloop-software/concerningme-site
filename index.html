Civilized Discussion

We strive to create an inclusive and safe environment for discussing the issues that matter most. We accomplish this by requiring everyone to abide by a set of ground rules.

ConcerningMe is based on a leading edge discussion platform called Discourse. Discourse is a 100% open source discussion platform built for the next decade of the Internet. It's a game changer when it comes to on-line discussion software.

Learn more about Discourse

Trust System

Our trust system encourages good behaviour by rewarding actions that benefit the community. These actions are as simple as reading topics, liking posts, and commenting.

As your reputation grows, you gain new abilities to participate and maintain the community.

Polls

Quickly check the pulse of the community by starting a poll and analysing the results.

Real Name Not Required

Some of the issues discussed here are sensitive which is why we don't require you to tell us who you are. We know there are some who hide behind anonymity to spread hatred and negativity, but don't worry, our trust and community moderation system will take care of them :).

Bring your friends

Once you’re trusted, you can click a button to invite your friends via email and they can reply to your topic with a single click. Staff can easily invite an entire group of people by uploading email addresses.
